Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026. Developer: Eric Slutz. Contact: support@blockiversevr.com.

Summary

Blockiverse VR is a single-player and local multiplayer voxel building game for Meta Quest. It is designed to collect as little personal data as possible. It does not include advertising, does not use third-party analytics SDKs, and does not sell personal data.

Information Blockiverse VR handles

  • Meta platform data: When you use Meta platform features, such as Meta Horizon avatars, store services, or Meta Quest party chat, those features are provided by Meta under Meta's own policies. Blockiverse VR uses only the minimum platform identifiers needed to display avatars and connect a session.
  • Local game data: World saves, player inventory, ruleset-defined world state, and comfort/settings preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • LAN multiplayer data: In local LAN multiplayer, devices exchange IP addresses and game state directly to host or join a session. This data is used for the active session and is not stored after the session ends.
  • Diagnostic logs: The game can write local diagnostic logs to help debug crashes and performance issues. Logs are stored on the device and are not automatically uploaded.

Information Blockiverse VR does not collect

  • No precise location data.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party analytics SDK data.
  • No sale or rental of personal data.
  • No in-app voice chat or in-app text chat.

Voice chat

Blockiverse VR does not include in-app voice chat. Voice communication between players uses Meta Quest party chat, which is governed by Meta's privacy policy.

Children's privacy

Blockiverse VR is not directed to children under the minimum age required by Meta's platform policies in your region. The game does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your choices

  • You can delete local game data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage Meta platform features through your Meta account and headset settings.
  • You can choose whether to share local diagnostic logs or save files when requesting support.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Blockiverse VR evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the last-updated date on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@blockiversevr.com or support@ericslutz.dev.
